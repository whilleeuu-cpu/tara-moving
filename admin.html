1. Services Included in Flat-Fee Price
The flat-fee price of $2,280 is all-inclusive and covers: full labor for loading and unloading, all protective packing materials (blankets, stretch wrap, floor runners), furniture disassembly and reassembly, specialty item handling (piano), tolls, fuel, and travel. No additional charges will be added on move day. The price is guaranteed and locked as of the contract signing date.
2. Client Rights & Protections (Legally Required — NY/NJ)
Under New York State regulations (NY Transportation Law §182) and New Jersey regulations (N.J.A.C. 13:44D), you as the client have the following rights:

Right to a binding estimate: This flat-fee quote constitutes a binding estimate. Tara Moving & Storage cannot charge more than the agreed amount except for services expressly requested by client after signing.

Right to your belongings: Tara Moving & Storage cannot hold your belongings hostage to collect additional charges beyond the agreed price.

Right to file a complaint: You may file a complaint with the New York State Department of Transportation (1-800-786-5423) or the New Jersey Division of Consumer Affairs (1-800-242-5846) if you believe your rights have been violated.

Right to a written inventory: You are entitled to a written inventory (Bill of Lading) of all items moved. This document serves as your receipt and proof of the contract.

Right to claim for damages: If any item is damaged during the move, you have 30 days to file a written claim with Tara Moving & Storage. The company carries full valuation coverage per USDOT regulations.
3. Client Responsibilities
The client agrees to: (a) ensure all items listed in the agreed inventory are ready and accessible at the scheduled pickup time; (b) disclose any special circumstances (narrow hallways, no elevator, parking restrictions) prior to the move date; (c) ensure proper access to both pickup and delivery addresses including elevator reservations if required; (d) be present or designate an authorized representative at both origin and destination; (e) sign the Bill of Lading (e-BOL) upon completion of delivery to confirm satisfactory service.
4. Company Obligations
Tara Moving & Storage agrees to: (a) provide a trained, background-checked crew led by Foreman ID FRM-1; (b) arrive within the agreed time window; (c) handle all items with professional care using appropriate protective materials; (d) provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) to the building management upon request; (e) provide real-time GPS tracking access to the client during the move; (f) assign a Move Coordinator available by phone throughout the move day; (g) not subcontract the work to third parties without client consent.
5. Liability & Insurance Coverage
Tara Moving & Storage carries Full Value Protection coverage as required by federal law (49 CFR Part 375). In the event of loss or damage, the company is liable for the full replacement value of the affected items, subject to the claims procedure outlined in Section 2. Additional insurance options are available upon request. The company's USDOT number is 4829104 and operates under MC# 1847293.
6. Cancellation Policy
Free cancellation: Up to 72 hours before the scheduled move date, cancellation is free of charge with full deposit refund.
Late cancellation (48–72 hours): 25% of the total price will be charged as a cancellation fee.
Last-minute cancellation (under 48 hours): 50% of the total price will be charged.
Same-day cancellation: 75% of the total price will be charged.
All refunds are processed within 3–5 business days via the original payment method.
7. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes arising from this agreement shall first be subject to a 30-day good-faith resolution period between the client and Tara Moving & Storage. If unresolved, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration in New York County under the rules of the American Arbitration Association. This agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
